About Me


Hey there! I'm Carter Gorski, a recent graduate from Saskatchewan Polytechnic's Computer Systems Technology program. Armed with a diverse skill set ranging from Python and Java to SQL and Swift, I've dived deep into everything tech. But it’s not just about the code for me, I bring a leadership background to the table too, having supervised a team of lifeguards at the University of Regina, where I honed my abilities to manage, lead, and stay cool under pressure.

Before diving into tech, I spent years ensuring safety as a lifeguard and teaching others the ropes, all while juggling responsibilities at places like Brandt as a saw operator, where I tackled manual labor and complex machinery. This mixture of tech and real-world experience makes me uniquely positioned to tackle challenging problems with a practical, hands-on approach.

I’m on the lookout for opportunities where I can put my technical and leadership skills to good use, enhancing systems, and creating solutions that make a real difference. Whether it’s writing clean code or ensuring project deadlines are met with precision, I'm ready to dive in and make waves. So, let's connect and see how we can make your next project a success!
